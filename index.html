Handling a Broad Range of Issues

Across the board, Bouhan Falligant’s experienced attorneys handle a broad range of complex legal issues for businesses, governmental entities, organizations, professionals, and individuals, both locally, regionally, and nationally.

We’ve been proud to represent a broad arrange of a diverse clientele in Savannah and the Coastal Empire, handling complex legal matters in litigation, estate planning, corporate law, commercial and residential real estate, admiralty, bankruptcy, maritime law, and other practice areas.

We are diligent and determined because we care about the success of our clients. We work closely with them to understand their needs and meet their objectives. Our philosophy has always been to make sure that our clients are successful and we are passionate in ensuring that happens.

Recognized not only in the courtroom but also by our peers, Bouhan Falligant’s experienced attorneys have received awards and positions of distinction in the legal field for professionalism, knowledge, and character; are regularly asked to lead continuing legal education seminars; and frequently write and present on legal topics.

We adhere to the highest standards of the legal profession and place the utmost value on integrity, a quality work product, and community service. Professionalism and ethics are at the core of everything we do.

More Than 130 Years of Experience

Bouhan Falligant is proud of our legacy of providing excellent legal service to clients throughout the Southeast and beyond for over 130 years. It is the successor firm to Bouhan, Williams & Levy LLP and Inglesby, Falligant, Horne, Courington & Chisholm P.C.

Over the years, Bouhan Falligant has witnessed our share of changes and has always emerged stronger and with greater resolve. The firm continues our tradition of providing a complete and diverse offering of legal services to our clients. Whether for individuals or business organizations, Bouhan Falligant will always offer a unique combination of time-tested experience from veteran attorneys and the innovation of younger members, providing clients with the distinct advantage that, as history has proven, will stand the test of time.
